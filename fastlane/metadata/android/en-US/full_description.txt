A Garmin-only fork of Gadgetbridge with a new look. It pairs with your Garmin watch over Bluetooth and keeps everything on your phone. No account, no vendor app, no cloud.

What's in it:

* Today screen with a steps ring and swipeable metric tiles
* Sleep with a score, stages and a 7-night trend
* Fitness and Health tabs you can customise
* Intensity minutes with a daily view (Garmin only shows weekly)
* Workouts, streaks and shareable cards
* Light/dark and accent colours

Built on Gadgetbridge's Garmin engine, so your data never leaves the phone (apart from optional weather).
